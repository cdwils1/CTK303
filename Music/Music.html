TOP CHARTS OF SEPTEMBER

D I L E M M A

Nelly featuring Kelly Rowland

The song "Dilemma" was featured on the album "Nellyville" is the second studio album by American rapper Nelly, released on June 25, 2002. The album was quite successful and debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 chart, which tracks the most popular albums in the United States. This achievement marked a significant milestone in Nelly's career and showcased his popularity and influence in the music industry at that time. Dilemma was one of the top streamed song on this album and allow Nellyville to stay a front runner on billboard for consecutive weeks.
