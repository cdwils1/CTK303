ISU Mission Statement

The mission of Illinois State University Athletic Performance is to cultivate a championship-level training environment in order to physically develop our student-athletes for the rigors of competition. This is accomplished by integrating each discipline within the Athletic Department to create a cohesive plan of preparation. We believe this will best allow student-athletes to reach their peak level of performance.
